The Salt Lake City Tattoo Expo is THE premier tattoo expo in the western US. The three day event is known as one of the most prestigious in the country; hosting over 150 of the most talented tattoo studios and featuring over 300 tattoo artists from around the globe.

All styles of tattooing will be available. Whether it be American Traditional, Black and Gray, Fine Line, Realism, or Japanese, we will be showcasing a lineup of the best tattoo artists the US and around the world in a safe, clean, and family friendly environment.
