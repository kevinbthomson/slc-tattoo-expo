The Salt Lake City International Tattoo Expo is THE premier tattoo expo in the western US, as well as one of the most prestigious in the country; hosting over 150 of the most talented tattoo artists from around the globe as well as artists and vendors.

All styles of tattooing will be featured. Whether it be American Traditional, Black and Gray, Fine Line, Realism, or Japanese, we will be showcasing a lineup of the best tattoo artists from Utah, the US and around the world in a safe, clean, and family friendly environment.
